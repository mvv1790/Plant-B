Our Philosophy

Welcome to Plant-B and thank you for stopping by. The idea behind this website is simple.
To take care of our planet and to use its natural resources sparingly. Arguably the best place to start with is to adjust our eating habits.
Here are some links to studies, "Which Diet Has the Least Environmental Impact on Our Planet? A Systematic Review of Vegan, Vegetarian and Omnivorous Diets" and
"Environmental impact of omnivorous, ovo-lacto-vegetarian, and vegan diet".Everbody is welcome to do their own research in order to learn more about the benefits of vegan diet.
